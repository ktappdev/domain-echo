# Privacy Policy for Domain Echo

**Last Updated:** February 9, 2026

## Introduction

Domain Echo ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Domain Echo Chrome extension.

## Information We Collect

### Chat Messages
- **What:** Text messages you send in chat rooms
- **Why:** To display messages to other users in the same domain's chat room
- **Storage:** Messages are stored in our database and associated with the domain you're visiting
- **Retention:** Messages are retained to maintain chat history for users

### Username
- **What:** Your chosen username or auto-generated anonymous name
- **Why:** To identify messages in the chat interface
- **Storage:** Stored locally in your browser and sent with each message
- **Control:** You can change your username at any time

### Domain Information
- **What:** The domain/website you're currently visiting
- **Why:** To connect you to the correct chat room for that domain
- **Storage:** Used only to route you to the appropriate chat room
- **Note:** We do NOT track your browsing history

### Presence Data
- **What:** Indication that you're currently active in a chat room
- **Why:** To show active user counts to other visitors
- **Storage:** Temporary - removed when you close the chat or leave the domain
- **Privacy:** No personal information is associated with presence data

## Information We DO NOT Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or process:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone, address)
- Payment or financial information
- Browsing history beyond the current domain
- Location data
- Device information
- IP addresses (beyond what's necessary for server communication)
- Cookies or tracking data

## How We Use Information

The information we collect is used solely for:
1. **Providing chat functionality** - Delivering messages between users
2. **Displaying usernames** - Showing who sent each message
3. **Showing active users** - Displaying how many people are in each chat room
4. **Maintaining chat rooms** - Organizing conversations by domain

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your messages with advertisers
- Use your data for marketing purposes
- Track you across websites
- Share data with analytics companies

Your chat messages are visible to:
- Other users in the same domain's chat room
- Our database infrastructure (for storage and delivery)

## Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:
- Encrypted connections (HTTPS/WSS)
- Secure database storage
- No unnecessary data collection
- Minimal permission requests

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Use anonymously** - No account or personal information required
- **Choose your username** - Use any name you prefer
- **Delete messages** - Contact us to request message deletion
- **Stop using the service** - Simply uninstall the extension

## Children's Privacy

Domain Echo does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The extension is intended for users 13 and older. If you believe a child has used our service, please contact us.

## Third-Party Services

Domain Echo uses:
- **Convex** - For real-time database and messaging infrastructure
  - Privacy Policy: https://www.convex.dev/privacy
  - They process chat messages and presence data on our behalf

We do not use:
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Tracking pixels

## Browser Permissions

Domain Echo requests these permissions:

### activeTab
- **Purpose:** To detect which domain you're visiting
- **Usage:** Only used to connect you to the correct chat room
- **Privacy:** We don't track or store your browsing history

### storage
- **Purpose:** To save your username preference locally
- **Usage:** Stored only in your browser, not sent to our servers
- **Privacy:** You can clear this data anytime through browser settings

## Data Retention

- **Chat messages:** Retained indefinitely to maintain chat history
- **Presence data:** Deleted immediately when you close the chat
- **Username preference:** Stored locally until you clear browser data
